SunPos application allows to query various information about sun:

  • Solar noon time and Julian day number
  • Ecliptic and equatorial coordinates
  • Azimuth, zenith and alevation angles
  • Sunrise and sunset time
  • Twilight and golden hour (photography) time
  • Day length and local sun coordinates
  • Sun solstice and equinox parameters
